Who I Am

A builder, a teammate,
and a relentless learner.

I'm Sanjula S, studying Computer Science and Engineering at NEC, graduating in 2028. My approach to learning has always been the same — build something real, break it, fix it, and understand why it broke. That mindset has taken me from writing my first REST API to contributing to a production-level team project, competing in a police department-organized hackathon, and earning a Global Nomination at NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025.

My technical interests sit at the intersection of full-stack development and data analytics. Most of my best work has happened in collaboration — debugging a module with a teammate, or whiteboarding a data model before a hackathon deadline. I'm currently strengthening my full-stack foundations through a live team project, sharpening analytics skills with Power BI, and always looking for the next meaningful problem to solve.
